⭐ Featured Article

The Kraft Singles Incident (also known as The Kraftening or The Donnybrook) was a watershed moment in Threat Level Midnight server history, occurring on January 21, 2023 in the 👔 Conference Room.

What began as a simple question about spelling—"Are you trying to say craft or Kraft?"—escalated into a full-scale philosophical debate about whether Kraft Singles constitute real cheese. The incident was so culturally significant that it led to the creation of a dedicated server emoji, an annual commemoration on January 21st, and is referenced to this day as a turning point in the server's culture.

Key participants included King Scubdu, Josh, Thaddy Thee Galleon, and Ianternet McAnus. The aftermath led Ianternet to a philosophical revelation: "Take nothing seriously."
