King Scubdu (username: u.s.marshall) is a member of the Threat Level Midnight Discord server and, by every quantifiable measure, its most prolific poster. With approximately 9,300 messages in the Conference Room alone — not counting his contributions to APC-21, Sandals Resort, and other channels — King Scubdu is the undisputed champion of having opinions and sharing them.

His role in the Kraft Singles Incident of January 21, 2023, secured his place in server mythology. He has since served as both the subject of Kraft-related ribbing and a philosophical voice in its retrospective analysis. He owns a Subaru. He worries about hail. He is, by all accounts, an essential element of TLM's social ecosystem.

Overview

King Scubdu joined TLM at an indeterminate point and immediately set about becoming the server's most active member. His posting habits span all categories: tech discussion, gaming, weather monitoring, food debate, sports, and extended riffs on any topic that catches his attention. He is not known for brevity.

Despite — or perhaps because of — his high volume output, King Scubdu is a central figure in the server's social life. He is one of the first to respond to new developments, one of the last to let a conversation die, and reliably present for every significant event the server has produced.

Known Aliases

  • King Scubdu — Primary display name
  • Scubdu — Common shorthand used by other members
  • The King — Informal honorific, used mostly by himself
  • u.s.marshall — Discord username; evokes both American law enforcement and a certain regal authority

Server Activity

King Scubdu's posting record is without peer in the server. He has contributed to virtually every channel and every era of TLM history. His presence in the conference room archive is so dominant that removing his messages would reduce the file by a significant fraction.

He is known for:

  • Starting discussions that expand far beyond their original scope
  • Offering strong opinions on technology, particularly hardware
  • Real-time weather reporting during Nebraska storm events
  • Maintaining awareness of his Subaru's wellbeing at all times

Role in The Kraftening

King Scubdu's role in the Kraft Singles Incident was foundational. When Josh asked for clarification on whether the intended word was "craft" or "Kraft," it was Scubdu who confirmed: "kraft." This single, three-word (or five-letter) confirmation set the entire debate in motion.

In the months following the incident, Scubdu demonstrated genuine self-awareness about its implications:

"And at the same time it's important to remember that a lot of the time we have no idea what someone will take a certain way or not, i.e. the Kraft Cheese incident."
— King Scubdu, July 10, 2023

He also maintained his characteristic dry wit when the incident resurfaced in November 2024:

"Sounds like somebody has a little Kraft single obsession."
— King Scubdu, to Ianternet, November 13, 2024

Interests and Topics

King Scubdu's conversational range is broad, but several topics emerge as consistent preoccupations:

  • Hardware and technology: GPU discussions, PC specs, tech news
  • Weather: Particularly Nebraska severe weather events and their effect on outdoor belongings
  • Vehicle ownership: His Subaru is a recurring character in his posts, especially during hail warnings
  • Sports: Contributes to Full Mast discussions
  • Server identity and culture: Often engages on meta-level conversations about the server itself
  • Gaming: PUBG era participant, ongoing gaming discussions

Notable Moments

The Subaru Hail Scare (May 7, 2023)

During a severe weather event in the Lincoln, NE area, King Scubdu expressed significant concern about potential hail damage to his Subaru and worry about tree branches near his property. The incident humanized him in a way that even his thousands of text messages had not fully accomplished.

The Kraft Anniversary Commentary

On multiple occasions, Scubdu has engaged thoughtfully with the legacy of the Kraft Incident — sometimes reflecting on it seriously, sometimes deflecting with humor. His dual role as both originator and analyst of The Kraftening makes him uniquely positioned in the server's historical narrative.
