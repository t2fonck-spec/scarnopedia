I'll Bring the Beards (username: rawrockkillz) is a member of Threat Level Midnight who has carved out a distinctive role as the server's primary technology news aggregator. With approximately 3,970 messages in the Conference Room, he is the server's fourth most prolific poster and its most reliable source of breaking tech news.

His display name — I'll Bring the Beards — is a commitment that has never been fulfilled, as beards are not deliverable via Discord. This has not dampened enthusiasm for the promise.

Overview

Where other members might stumble into tech discussions incidentally, I'll Bring the Beards approaches it systematically. He regularly posts links to technology articles and commentary via FXTwitter embeds, covering topics including:

  • AI and machine learning developments
  • GPU pricing and availability
  • Social media platform changes
  • Software releases and updates
  • General internet culture and tech industry news

This consistent behavior has made him the de facto Reuters wire service of TLM's tech coverage. If something happened in the technology world, Beards probably already posted about it in APC-21 or the Conference Room.

Notable Contributions

Beyond tech news, I'll Bring the Beards is a consistent presence in general server discussion. He contributes to gaming conversations, participates in the broader social life of the server, and occasionally offers his own commentary on the news he shares rather than just posting links.

His username "rawrockkillz" suggests a musical background or persona — possibly hard rock or metal — that contrasts interestingly with his role as the server's measured, curated tech correspondent.

Legacy

In a server populated by passionate debaters and extreme reactors, I'll Bring the Beards provides a distinct form of value: consistent, reliable information sharing. He is the member you go to when you need to know what actually happened in the tech world this week. His beard delivery remains pending.
