Josh (username: baneofbelial, User ID: 144184828172959745) is a member of Threat Level Midnight and one of the most historically significant figures in the server's cultural life. He is best known for asking the question that triggered the Kraft Singles Incident of January 21, 2023, and for his subsequent role as the self-appointed historian and annual commemorator of that event.

Josh occupies a particular niche in TLM lore: the careful, precise member who notices discrepancies, asks for clarification, and then watches in alarm as that clarification request ignites a multi-year cultural controversy. He did not start the cheese debate so much as he opened the door through which it walked.

Overview

Josh's username, "baneofbelial," is a reference to the demonic figure Belial from Judeo-Christian tradition. Whether Josh considers himself a bane to demons, or simply liked how it sounded, has not been recorded. What has been recorded is that he is a thoughtful, engaged member who participates actively in server discussions and takes the preservation of server history seriously.

The Question That Started It All

On January 21, 2023, Josh posed what would become the most consequential question in TLM history. Noticing ambiguity in the discussion about cheese and beer, he asked:

"Are you trying to say craft or Kraft?"
— Josh, January 21, 2023 — The Question

In fairness to Josh, this was a perfectly reasonable request for clarification. The words "craft" and "Kraft" are homophones in American English, and distinguishing between artisanal beer cheese and processed American cheese slices is genuinely important for any culinary discussion. Josh did nothing wrong. He simply asked a question.

What followed was not his fault. Probably.

In the aftermath, Josh assessed the incident with characteristic gravity:

"The Kraft incident was a dark day indeed."
— Josh, July 10, 2023

Keeper of Kraft Singles Day

In the years since The Kraftening, Josh has emerged as the server's primary commemorator of the event. He has reliably marked the anniversary on January 21st of each subsequent year, ensuring that the incident is never forgotten:

"Guys, today is the 2 year anniversary of the Kraftening."
— Josh, January 21, 2025
"happy Kraft Singles day everyone"
— Josh, January 21, 2026

This annual observance has established Josh as the unofficial steward of the server's historical consciousness — the member who ensures that TLM does not forget where it came from, or what it argued about when it got there.

The Allergy Question

Server archives contain multiple references to Josh having a peanut or nut allergy, which his fellow members have referenced with characteristic TLM sensitivity (i.e., as recurring joke material). The exact nature and severity of the allergy are not documented in Scarnopedia, but its existence appears well-established in the server's collective awareness.

Notably, the allergy has not prevented Josh from engaging in detailed discussions about food, cooking, and cheese. He is, by all accounts, a thoughtful eater.

Interests

  • Historical preservation: Josh has demonstrated genuine commitment to remembering and honoring server history
  • Semantic precision: His instinct to clarify "craft vs. Kraft" reveals a mind attuned to the importance of precise language
  • General server discourse: Active participant across multiple channels
  • Storm monitoring: Expressed concern during the May 2023 weather events
