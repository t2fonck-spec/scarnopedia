Goffers (username: goffers, User ID: 287247440447406080) is a member of Threat Level Midnight best known for a single, devastating five-word statement in response to the Kraft Singles Incident:

"Haven't bought Kraft since."
— Goffers

This statement — delivered at some point after The Kraftening of January 21, 2023 — represents the most concrete, real-world consequence of the incident. While other members processed The Kraftening through philosophical reflection (Ianternet), annual commemoration (Josh), or dry humor (King Scubdu), Goffers took action. He stopped buying Kraft.

The Boycott

Goffers' Kraft boycott is remarkable for its practical scope. The debate of January 21, 2023 centered on whether Kraft Singles are real cheese. Goffers apparently concluded — or confirmed an existing belief — that they are not, and adjusted his grocery purchasing accordingly.

The duration and current status of the boycott are unknown. No subsequent messages have confirmed or denied whether Goffers has resumed Kraft purchases. The boycott is assumed to be ongoing until evidence to the contrary emerges.

Standing in the Server

Despite the brevity of his most famous contribution, Goffers is a genuine member of TLM's community with a user ID suggesting tenure predating several more prolific posters. He represents a category of server member whose impact is disproportionate to their message count — the quality-over-quantity school of Discord participation.

In the historiography of The Kraftening, Goffers stands apart as the member who demonstrates that server arguments can have lasting behavioral consequences. He is, in a sense, the most serious person in a discussion that everyone else eventually turned into a joke.
