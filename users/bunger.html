bUNGER (real name: Ben; username: benearlweber, also known as benwiththelens, User ID: 387770515286392847) is the administrator of Threat Level Midnight and the server's closest approximation to a founding father. He is responsible for the architecture of the server's rules, the maintenance of its culture, and the occasional delivery of devastating historical recaps when members forget their own history.

With approximately 7,112 messages in the Conference Room, bUNGER is the server's second most prolific poster, trailing only King Scubdu. He is a member of the BrBa (Breaking Bad) clan, which may explain certain things about his approach to conflict resolution.

Overview

bUNGER occupies a unique position in TLM: he is simultaneously a peer and an authority figure. As administrator, he sets and enforces the rules. As a member, he participates in the same casual, chaotic discourse as everyone else. This dual role has given him a perspective on server history that no other member can fully claim.

He is known for his wit, his administrative precision, and his ability to deliver devastating one-line historical summaries of events that other members have conveniently "forgotten."

Administrative Role

As server administrator, bUNGER is responsible for:

  • Creating and updating server rules
  • Managing channel structure and categories
  • Enforcing moderation policies
  • Serving as the institutional memory of the server when members selectively forget their transgressions

Authorship of Server Rules

In January 2026, bUNGER authored (or updated) the comprehensive server rules posted in the Welcome and Server Rules channel. These rules include several characteristically TLM policies:

  • The "Good Faith" Standard: Zero tolerance for slurs; all members must operate in good faith.
  • No Trolling/Baiting Rule: Provocative behavior for its own sake is prohibited.
  • Apolitical Zone: No political discussions in public channels. Violators receive a 24-hour timeout.
  • The "Touch Grass" Protocol: After heated arguments, members must take a mandatory 1-hour break.
  • The "Sloppy Croppy" Decree: Screenshots must be properly cropped. This is non-negotiable.
  • NSFW Prohibition: Standard.

The "Sloppy Croppy" decree is widely considered the most TLM-specific rule ever written and speaks to bUNGER's deep appreciation for visual presentation standards.

Kraft Incident Involvement

bUNGER's most notable contribution to The Kraftening legacy came not during the original incident but in its aftermath. On October 13, 2023, he delivered what is now considered the definitive summary of the event:

"Bro stfu you're the one who went on a rant about how Kraft singles weren't technically cheese so you'd never be caught dead ingesting it."
— bUNGER, to the guilty party, October 13, 2023

This quote confirms that the core of The Kraftening was a genuine argument about the dairy classification of processed cheese slices — not merely a semantic debate about spelling — and identifies at least one participant who took a hardline anti-Kraft stance.
