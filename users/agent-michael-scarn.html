Agent Michael Scarn (username: billgrip) is a member of Threat Level Midnight holding the rank of Upper Management — a title appropriate for someone who shares a name with the greatest fictional secret agent in the history of Dunder Mifflin Paper Company.

The server itself — Threat Level Midnight — takes its name from the spy film written, directed, produced, and starred in by Michael Scott in The Office. Agent Michael Scarn is, therefore, the member who most fully embodies the server's entire thematic identity. He is the living avatar of TLM's spirit.

The Name

Michael Scarn is the fictional secret agent alter ego of Michael Scott, the hapless regional manager of Dunder Mifflin Scranton in the NBC series The Office. Michael Scott spent years writing, producing, and eventually screening a feature film called Threat Level Midnight, in which he starred as Agent Scarn.

The server's decision to name itself "Threat Level Midnight" — and to use The Office terminology throughout (Scranton Branch, Conference Room, Corporate, Finer Things Club) — means that Agent Michael Scarn, the member, is the human personification of TLM's organizing mythology.

The PUBG Era

During the server's early PUBG Era, Agent Michael Scarn was an active participant in squad formations and battle royale sessions. He is noted for having celebrated games in which he achieved 2–3 kills as significant personal achievements — a detail that was recorded not mockingly but with the genuine warmth of a community that appreciated growth and effort regardless of outcome.

"I'm not sure how to tell how many kills we got as a squad, but I know it was several."
— A member, possibly Agent Michael Scarn, on the complexity of PUBG statistics

Upper Management

Agent Michael Scarn's server role of "Upper Management" is consistent with the Office-themed hierarchy of TLM. He operates above the Scranton Branch level, occupying a position of authority that mirrors Michael Scott's actual position at Dunder Mifflin — though presumably with more effective results.
