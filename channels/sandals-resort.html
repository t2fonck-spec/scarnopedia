Sandals Resort (formally 🌴-sandals-resort, Channel ID: 814686630328664075) is a Scranton Branch channel of Threat Level Midnight that serves primarily as the planning and logistics hub for a server cabin retreat. With approximately 34 MB of archived content, it is one of the largest channels in the TLM archive — a testament to how seriously the membership takes retreat planning.

The channel's name is a reference to the Chris Farley sketch in which he plays a motivational speaker who lives in a van, often threatening to have members living in "a van down by the river." TLM adapted this into a cabin-by-the-river aesthetic, christened "Sandals Resort" with maximum irony.

Overview

Sandals Resort functions as the operational nerve center for the server's aspirational in-person gathering. The planning documented in the channel is comprehensive, covering:

  • Date selection: Members voted on multiple proposed dates, with May 1st winning
  • Activity planning: Shooting, swimming, general outdoor activities
  • Documentation: Plans to film a documentary of the event
  • Provisions: Alcohol and other supplies
  • Firearms logistics: Discussions about guns for recreational shooting

The Retreat Plan

The planned cabin retreat represents one of TLM's most ambitious real-world coordination efforts. The concept of a group of Discord friends organizing an actual in-person gathering — with scheduled activities, democratically selected dates, and a documentary crew — speaks to the depth of the relationships formed in the server.

"This is the cabin down by the river retreat thread."
— Channel founding statement

The retreat plan involved enough detail to suggest serious intent, including discussion of what firearms to bring for target shooting, what swimming activities were available, and how to film the event for posterity.

Whether the retreat actually occurred is left as an exercise for the reader. The Scarnopedia editorial board has been unable to confirm or deny the event's completion.

Channel Culture

Beyond retreat planning, Sandals Resort functions as a general casual hangout channel — consistent with its "resort" branding. The high message volume suggests it has become a go-to space for the lighter, more social interactions that define the TLM community at its most relaxed.
