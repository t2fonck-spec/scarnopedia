Big Gamer Time (formally 🎮-big-gamer-time, Channel ID: 847305438646370354) is a Finer Things Club channel of Threat Level Midnight dedicated to gaming discussion. It is, as the name indicates, when it is time to be a big gamer. The channel provides gaming news, recommendations, modding discussions, and — most importantly — alerts about free games on the Epic Games Store.

Overview

Big Gamer Time functions as TLM's gaming news desk. Members post updates on new releases, share game recommendations, discuss mods, and maintain a consistent log of free game offerings from the Epic Games Store. The channel's 24 MB of content reflects years of sustained gaming discussion.

Epic Games Store Free Game Tracking

One of Big Gamer Time's most practically useful functions is its tracking of the Epic Games Store's weekly free game offerings. EGS free games are posted and documented, allowing members to claim games they might otherwise miss. This service has likely resulted in TLM members collectively acquiring hundreds of free games over the channel's lifetime — many of which may never be played, but that is beside the point.

Gaming Discussions

Beyond free games, Big Gamer Time covers:

  • Game recommendations: Members share what they're playing and suggest titles to each other
  • Modding discussions: Modifications for PC games are discussed and shared
  • News and releases: Major gaming industry news filters through the channel

Relationship to the PUBG Era

Big Gamer Time exists as the institutional successor to the gaming discussions that once filled the Conference Room during the PUBG Era. As gaming discussion became more specialized, it found a dedicated home in the Finer Things Club, allowing Big Gamer Time to serve those members for whom gaming is, indeed, a finer thing.
