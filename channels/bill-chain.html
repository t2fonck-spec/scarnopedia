The Bill Chain (formally 🏦-the-bill-chain, Channel ID: 900823198738837615) is the Corporate-category channel of Threat Level Midnight that manages the server's internal economy. It is the home of Billcoin — TLM's proprietary digital currency — and the transactions, bets, predictions, and rankings that give the economy meaning.

The Bill Chain represents TLM at its most organized: a gamified reward system layered on top of the natural activities of server life (sports watching, gaming, working out), creating a parallel economy of achievement and competition.

The Billcoin Economy

Billcoin is the internal currency of TLM, earned and spent through a variety of activities tracked in The Bill Chain. The economy encompasses:

  • Sports Betting: Members wager Billcoin on sporting events, particularly Iowa and college football games. Bets are placed in the thousands of Billcoin, suggesting either extreme confidence or extreme recklessness.
  • Score Prediction Contests: Members predict game scores and receive Billcoin rewards for accuracy.
  • Workout Rewards: Physical fitness is incentivized through the economy. Completing a workout earns between 500–1,000 Billcoin — making exercise literally pay off within the TLM ecosystem.
  • Booster Rewards: Monthly server boosts and first-time boosting actions are tracked and rewarded.

Wealth Distribution

The Bill Chain maintains records of member wealth, creating a Billcoin leaderboard. Archive references suggest uneven wealth distribution, with at least one member ("Reed") noted as having fallen to 9th place in the rankings due to infrequent check-ins — a cautionary tale about the importance of staying engaged with the TLM economy.

Cultural Significance

The existence of the Billcoin economy reflects TLM's broader community philosophy: activities that members do anyway (watch sports, exercise, game) are given additional meaning through gamification. The Bill Chain transforms passive viewing into active participation and rewards real-world physical effort with in-server status.
