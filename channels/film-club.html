The TLM Film Club (formally 🎥-tlm-film-club) is the cinematic discussion channel of Threat Level Midnight, housed within the Finer Things Club category. It provides a dedicated space for members who believe that movies — much like craft beer and not Kraft Singles — deserve to be taken seriously.

The channel's placement in the Finer Things Club is significant: film, in TLM's organizational schema, is elevated alongside sports (Full Mast) and gaming (Big Gamer Time) as a pursuit worthy of dedicated subcommunity status.

The Finer Things Club Connection

The Finer Things Club is itself a reference to The Office, where a group of Dunder Mifflin employees formed an exclusive club for appreciation of art, literature, and cinema. TLM's Finer Things Club category carries the same energy: it is for the members who want to go deep on their interests rather than merely mentioning them in the Conference Room.
