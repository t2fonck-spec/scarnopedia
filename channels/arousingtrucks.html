Arousingtrucks (formally 🧿👄🧿-arousingtrucks, Channel ID: 553602711426301955) is a Scranton Branch channel of Threat Level Midnight dedicated to the appreciation of semi-trucks, tractors, and other large vehicles in a manner that the channel name very strongly implies. It is exactly what it sounds like, and it is glorious.

With approximately 27 MB of archived content, Arousingtrucks is one of the more active Scranton Branch channels and the only one that has successfully spawned a dedicated Reddit presence.

Overview

Arousingtrucks occupies a unique niche in TLM's channel ecosystem. Unlike the Conference Room's general discourse or Memememes' image sharing, Arousingtrucks has a singular, focused identity: trucks. Big trucks. Trucks that are, per the channel's thesis, arousing.

The channel's emoji name — 🧿👄🧿 — has never been fully decoded, but the combination of eyes and a mouth in this context is generally interpreted as an appreciative expression. The 🧿 (evil eye) emojis may serve as protective wards against the overwhelming emotional response to particularly good trucks.

Channel Description

The channel serves as a hub for sharing images, videos, and commentary about trucks and related heavy machinery. Members contribute photos of impressive semi-trucks, post appreciative reactions to others' finds, and engage in genuine vehicular discourse. The vibe is consistently one of shared enthusiasm.

Beyond imagery, Arousingtrucks has hosted substantive discussions about truck culture, the trucking industry, and the unique aesthetic appeal of large commercial vehicles. It operates with the earnestness of a genuine fan community.

The Reddit Account

The channel's most significant external achievement was the creation of the Reddit account PM_ME_SEXY_TRUCK_PIX — a username that requires no further explanation. The account was created as a direct extension of the Arousingtrucks channel philosophy, bringing TLM's truck appreciation ethos to a wider audience.

The account's existence represents a milestone in TLM history: the moment the server's internal culture successfully crossed into a public platform. See: The Reddit Account.

Tractor Tuesday

Tractor Tuesday is a recurring observance originating in Arousingtrucks in which members post tractor-related content every Tuesday. It is the channel's most formally structured tradition and demonstrates the community's commitment to agricultural vehicle appreciation as a sustained practice rather than a one-time novelty.

The success of Tractor Tuesday in establishing a weekly rhythm suggests that the channel's membership takes its tractor appreciation seriously and would like to keep it on schedule.

The Jack Situation

Server archives contain multiple references to a member named Jack in the context of Arousingtrucks, accompanied by expressions of concern about his age. The exact nature of the situation is handled with appropriate care in this encyclopedia: it is noted that age verification concerns were raised in this channel, that they were addressed with the characteristic dark humor of TLM, and that the matter appears to have resolved itself over time.

Culture

Arousingtrucks has an unusually coherent culture for a channel of its type. Members share a genuine appreciation for large vehicles, a willingness to engage with the inherent absurdity of the channel's premise, and a commitment to the Tractor Tuesday schedule. It is, in short, a community — one built around an unusual but entirely legitimate interest.

"We will take over the TRI-CITY AREA."
— An Arousingtrucks member, on the channel's territorial ambitions
