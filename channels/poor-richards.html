Poor Richards (named after the bar from The Office) is the culinary heart of Threat Level Midnight. With 6,191 messages spanning nearly five years, it is one of the server's most active lifestyle channels — a rolling feast of food photography, recipe sharing, restaurant reviews, and impassioned condiment debates. If TLM has a kitchen, this is it, and everyone is crowded around the stove arguing about beans.

Overview

Poor Richards serves as the server's primary venue for all food-adjacent conversation. The channel's top contributors — bUNGER (955), King Scubdu (936), Thaddy Thee Galleon (929), I'll Bring the Beards (760), and Ianternet (601) — represent a near-even distribution of posting, suggesting that food is one of the few topics on which TLM achieves something approaching democratic participation. RudeLilSquarepants rounds out the top tier at 555 messages, contributing a disproportionate share of the channel's most quotable material.

The Great Condiment Schisms

No food channel would be complete without its holy wars. Poor Richards has seen multiple condiment-related controversies, most notably the ranch vs. blue cheese debate that erupted after RudeLilSquarepants declared their allegiance:

"Ranch is alright but blue cheese is where its at"
— RudeLilSquarepants, drawing a line in the sand

This statement, as with most condiment opinions, was treated as a declaration of war by some and a statement of obvious truth by others.

Culinary Highlights

The channel documents a wide range of culinary adventures, from I'll Bring the Beards' family breakfast prep sessions to the persistent and inexplicable recurrence of bean photography. Members regularly share images of their meals, cooking projects, and restaurant visits, creating what amounts to a longitudinal study of TLM's collective diet.

"I freaking want sushi now"
— I'll Bring the Beards, speaking for all of us
"1 swiss mushroom burger no mushroom please"
— RudeLilSquarepants, ordering with the confidence of a man who knows what he wants (minus the mushroom)

Notable Moments

  • Bean Discourse — A recurring motif in which members photograph, discuss, and philosophize about beans with an intensity that borders on the academic
  • The Arsenic Incident — Agent Michael Scarn's casual confession that he likes his "gummy clusters with extra arsenic" raised eyebrows but no actual health concerns
  • Yia Yia's Controversy — Trenton's assertion that "yia yia's isnt really that good" was received with the gravity typically reserved for restaurant closures
  • The Pompatus of Love — King Scubdu asked Jack if he speaks of the pompatus of love, a question that remains unanswered by food science
"I like my gummy clusters with extra arsenic"
— Agent Michael Scarn, whose snacking habits warrant further investigation

Cultural Significance

Poor Richards is arguably TLM's most universally accessible channel. While other channels require specialized interests — gaming knowledge, sports fandom, or tolerance for Arousingtrucks — everyone eats. The channel's sustained activity since 2021 confirms what sociologists have long suspected: food is the ultimate social lubricant, even in digital spaces.
