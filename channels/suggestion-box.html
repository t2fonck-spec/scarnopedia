The Scranton Branch Suggestion Box (formally 🗑-scranton-branch-suggestion-box) is a Corporate-category channel of Threat Level Midnight. It is named after the garbage can that Michael Scott in The Office used as a literal suggestion box, which was then promptly ignored. The parallel to this channel's real-world usage is left as an observation rather than an accusation.

The channel has recorded approximately 1.6 MB of content from only 6 unique users. This may reflect genuine restraint on the part of TLM members in not overloading the feedback mechanism, or it may reflect a widely shared understanding that the 🗑 emoji was not chosen as a positive omen for suggestion outcomes.
