Memememes (formally 🍆-memememes, Channel ID: 554076427339825152) is the designated meme repository of Threat Level Midnight. The channel name features three "me"s rather than two — a deliberate stylistic choice that communicates something important about the channel's approach to language and tradition. The emoji designation of 🍆 communicates something else entirely.

Overview

Memememes serves as TLM's primary image board. Members share gaming memes, internet culture references, reaction images, and other visual humor. The channel has accumulated approximately 28 MB of content, suggesting a healthy and sustained flow of meme traffic.

Notable Content Categories

  • Gaming Memes: PC gaming culture, console wars, specific game references
  • Area 51 Content: The September 2019 Area 51 raid meme era appears in the archive, grounding Memememes in specific internet cultural moments
  • Marvel/Avengers: MCU-related humor and reaction images
  • PC Master Race: The classic PC vs. console debate meme format appears with notable frequency
  • Doctor Strange: References suggesting this character was a popular meme subject among members
  • Chuck E. Cheese: References to the family entertainment brand appear in contextually interesting ways

Reaction Culture

Memememes is home to some of TLM's most extensive emoji reaction use. The "THIS" and "Agreenis" custom emojis appear frequently as members react to posted images. The channel has a vote-up/vote-down dynamic driven entirely by emoji reactions rather than text responses.

Channel Identity

The 🍆 emoji as a channel identifier is a choice that Scarnopedia will not attempt to contextualize. The channel is what it is. The memes are what they are. The eggplant speaks for itself.
