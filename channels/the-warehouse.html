The Warehouse (formally 📦-the-warehouse, Channel ID: 553075685481250846) is a Corporate category channel of Threat Level Midnight. Named after the warehouse of Dunder Mifflin Scranton, where Darryl Philbin and the warehouse crew operated beneath the office workers, this channel occupies the lowest rung of TLM's corporate hierarchy.

Function

The Warehouse's exact purpose within the TLM server structure is unclear, as its archived export contains no messages. It may have been intended as a general access or introductory channel, or perhaps as a space for newer members before they gained access to the broader server — the Discord equivalent of starting in the mailroom.

The Office Hierarchy

Together with the Corporate Office (📈) and Middle Management (📉), The Warehouse completes TLM's three-tier corporate structure. That all three administrative channels are empty while the actual community channels overflow with content is perhaps the most authentic recreation of corporate dynamics ever achieved in a Discord server.

📌 This article is a stub. The Warehouse contains no archived messages. Like the warehouse on The Office, the real action happens upstairs.
