Corporate Office (formally 📈-corporate-office, Channel ID: 1465055152586358879) is a Corporate category channel of Threat Level Midnight. It exists in the server hierarchy as a designated space for upper management communication, though its archived export contains no messages — making it perhaps the most realistic corporate simulation in TLM history.

Function

The Corporate Office appears to serve as an administrative channel within TLM's corporate structure. Its placement in the Corporate category alongside Announcements and Middle Management suggests it was intended as a space for server leadership to coordinate. That it contains no messages is either a testament to the efficiency of TLM's leadership or an indictment of it — scholars remain divided.

📌 This article is a stub. The Corporate Office contains no archived messages. Like most corporate offices, its value appears to be largely symbolic.
