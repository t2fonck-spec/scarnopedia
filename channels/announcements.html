Announcements (formally 📢-announcements, Channel ID: 718656417610858507) is the official broadcast channel of Threat Level Midnight. It serves as the conduit through which administrative information — rule updates, role changes, server structure modifications — flows from management to membership.

Function

The Announcements channel is the TLM equivalent of a corporate memo board. Key announcements documented in its archive include:

  • Server rule updates (most recently January 2026)
  • Role creation and assignment notices
  • Server structural changes
  • Administrative decisions affecting the broader membership

The January 2026 Rule Update

The most significant documented use of the Announcements channel is the publication of the comprehensive server rules in January 2026, authored by bUNGER. These rules — including the "Touch Grass" protocol, the "Sloppy Croppy" decree, and the Apolitical Zone policy — represent the server's formal articulation of its community standards.
