Middle Management (formally 📉-middle-management, Channel ID: 718646791699955712) is a Corporate category channel of Threat Level Midnight. Positioned between the Corporate Office and the general membership, it occupies the organizational layer that every workplace has but nobody can quite explain the purpose of.

Function

Middle Management appears to be a restricted channel intended for moderators or members holding intermediate authority within TLM's hierarchy. Its export contains no messages, which — depending on one's perspective — either means the moderators are handling everything smoothly through other channels, or that middle management is performing exactly as middle management traditionally does.

The Office Parallel

In keeping with TLM's The Office naming conventions, the 📉 emoji choice for Middle Management is a characteristically self-aware touch. Where the Corporate Office gets 📈 (stonks going up), Middle Management receives 📉 (stonks going down) — a visual hierarchy that communicates more about organizational structure than any org chart could.
