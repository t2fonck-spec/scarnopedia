Get Swole (formally 💪-get-swole, Channel ID: 796960322790490122) is a Scranton Branch channel of Threat Level Midnight dedicated to fitness, weight loss challenges, and the eternal pursuit of gains. With a channel topic of simply "get swole," it communicates its mission with the kind of brevity normally associated with ancient Spartan warriors or people who are too out of breath to type longer sentences.

Overview

Get Swole serves as TLM's gym, if the gym had a group chat and nobody could actually spot you. The channel hosts weight loss challenges, workout accountability, diet tracking, progress photos, and the kind of motivational speech that can only come from people who understand what it's like to eat four tacos on a Saturday and then remember it was supposed to be a diet day.

Weight Challenges

The channel's primary organized activity revolves around weight loss competitions, complete with monetary stakes. Members put real money on the line — with pots reaching at least $40 — creating an economic incentive structure where losing weight literally means winning money. The challenges involve weigh-ins, progress tracking, and the collective accountability of knowing your friends can see whether you've been to the gym.

"Oh shit i forgot it was Saturday and I just ate like 4 tacos"
— A member, discovering that calendar awareness is a key component of dieting

Fitness Philosophy

Get Swole's approach to fitness spans a wide range of methodologies:

  • Intermittent fasting — Multiple members track fasting windows using dedicated apps
  • Calorie tracking — Apps like Lose It are used to monitor food intake, with reported success of 30+ pounds lost through consistency
  • 75 Hard — The viral fitness challenge has found devotees in the channel, with at least one member completing it and immediately wanting to restart
  • Rucking — Members have proposed group ruck events, combining fitness with the camaraderie of walking long distances while carrying heavy things
  • Wrestling weight cutting techniques — Because sometimes you need results and you need them by Friday
"maybe hulk pick up bigger rock for make body smaller"
— A member, distilling the entire fitness industry into nine words

Motivation and Accountability

The channel functions as a mutual support system where members hold each other accountable. Messages range from earnest encouragement ("You made the gym your bitch today. LFG") to gentle reminders ("Don't forget to hydrate") to philosophical observations about the nature of physical fitness ("The gym is not all mental"). One member's plea — "Everyone don't let me not work out this week lol" — captures the channel's core dynamic: people who want to get fit and need their friends to make sure they actually do it.

Progress Pics and AI Filters

Members share progress photos to document their fitness journeys. In a characteristically TLM twist, someone suggested running progress pics through TikTok's AI anime filter, because if you're going to transform your body, you might as well see what you'd look like as a shonen protagonist while you're at it.

Cultural Significance

Get Swole represents TLM at its most earnestly supportive. While much of the server thrives on irony and sarcasm, the fitness channel is where members genuinely root for each other's health and wellbeing — though they still can't resist cracking jokes about it. The channel proves that a Discord server originally built around gaming and memes can also be a place where people lose weight, build muscle, and occasionally draw GPS art of penises on their running routes.
