The Kraft Singles Incident, colloquially referred to as The Kraftening or The Donnybrook, was a pivotal moment in Threat Level Midnight server history that occurred on January 21, 2023. The incident began as a seemingly innocuous semantic argument in the 👔 Conference Room over whether Kraft Singles constitute real cheese and whether the word being referenced was "craft" (as in craft beer) or "Kraft" (as in the processed cheese product). The resulting debate was so culturally significant that it fundamentally altered the social fabric of the server and is now commemorated annually as Kraft Singles Day.

The incident has since been referenced as both a cautionary tale about the dangers of online miscommunication and a formative moment that revealed deep philosophical divisions within the membership regarding dairy products and their classification.

Background

Prior to the incident, the Conference Room had been a relatively stable forum for general discussion. Members had developed a casual shorthand and a level of social trust that, in retrospect, may have led to the complacency that allowed The Kraftening to occur.

In the weeks leading up to January 21, 2023, discussions in the channel had touched on topics including craft beer, cooking, and food in general. The conceptual proximity of "craft" (artisanal) and "Kraft" (the cheese brand) created a powder keg of linguistic ambiguity that would eventually detonate.

Historians note that the term "Donnybrook" — meaning a heated argument or brawl — was itself discussed in the server around this period, in what now appears to be a prophetic contextual coincidence. The word would later become one of the primary ways members referred to the incident.

The Incident

The Spark

The Kraft Singles Incident was ignited by a question that, in isolation, appears entirely reasonable. Josh (username: baneofbelial), noticing apparent ambiguity in a message about cheese, posed the following:

"Are you trying to say craft or Kraft?"
— Josh, January 21, 2023

This simple question opened a Pandora's Box. King Scubdu confirmed the reference was to "Kraft," setting off a chain of responses that would consume the channel for an indeterminate period.

"kraft"
— King Scubdu, in response

Escalation

Thaddy Thee Galleon immediately escalated the discourse by invoking the concept of "craft beer cheese," drawing a direct comparison between artisanal and processed cheese products:

"craft beer cheese"
— Thaddy Thee Galleon

Ianternet McAnus, demonstrating the sharp binary thinking that would become his signature contribution, issued what many scholars consider the definitive ruling of the incident:

"craft beer cheese: good / kraft beer cheese: bad"
— Ianternet "Opdick" McAnus

Thaddy then introduced the crowning synthesis of the debate — a visionary culinary concept that blurred the very line between craft and Kraft:

"gonna make kraft mac and cheese with a craft beer someday"
— Thaddy Thee Galleon

This statement — combining both a Kraft product (mac and cheese) with a craft product (beer) — was widely interpreted as either a stroke of genius or a deliberate provocation. Accounts differ.

Immediate Aftermath

The immediate aftermath of the core debate saw the channel shift into a period of processing. Members attempted to determine what, exactly, had just happened and who, if anyone, was responsible.

King Scubdu offered what would prove to be a surprisingly self-aware retrospective months later, in July 2023:

"And at the same time it's important to remember that a lot of the time we have no idea what someone will take a certain way or not, i.e. the Kraft Cheese incident."
— King Scubdu, July 10, 2023

This prompted Ianternet to share the philosophical transformation the incident had wrought upon him:

"That incident led me to a perspective change in this server: take nothing seriously."
— Ianternet McAnus, July 10, 2023

Cultural Impact

The Kraft Singles Incident is widely considered the most culturally significant event in TLM server history, surpassing even the PUBG Era in terms of lasting philosophical impact. Its effects can be summarized as follows:

  • Custom Emoji Creation: A dedicated Kraft Singles emoji was commissioned and added to the server, allowing members to react to cheese-adjacent content with appropriate gravity.
  • Lexical Enrichment: The word "Donnybrook" entered the active TLM vocabulary specifically as a result of this incident.
  • Philosophical Shift: Ianternet McAnus publicly credited the incident with permanently altering his approach to online discourse.
  • Kraft Boycott: At least one member, Goffers, reportedly ceased purchasing Kraft products as a direct consequence.
"The Kraft incident was a dark day indeed."
— Josh, July 10, 2023
"Haven't bought Kraft since."
— Goffers, on the lasting personal consequences

Annual Commemoration — Kraft Singles Day

The Kraft Singles Incident has achieved the rare distinction of becoming a recurring annual observance within the server. Each January 21st is now marked as Kraft Singles Day, a date of solemn reflection and cheese-adjacent humor.

January 21, 2023

The original incident occurs in Conference Room. The discourse rages. Cheese is questioned.

July 10, 2023

First major retrospective discussion. King Scubdu reflects; Ianternet reveals his philosophical transformation; Josh delivers his "dark day" verdict.

October 13, 2023

bUNGER delivers an impromptu historical recap of the incident: "Bro stfu you're the one who went on a rant about how Kraft singles weren't technically cheese so you'd never be caught dead ingesting it."

November 13, 2024

Ianternet raises the incident unprompted: "Is anybody else ever randomly think about the craft singles donnybrook?" King Scubdu responds: "Sounds like somebody has a little Kraft single obsession."

January 21, 2025

Josh marks the two-year anniversary: "Guys, today is the 2 year anniversary of the Kraftening." The name The Kraftening is formally adopted.

January 21, 2026

Josh returns for year three: "happy Kraft Singles day everyone." The tradition is now fully established.

Legacy

The Kraft Singles Incident endures as the defining cultural reference point of the Threat Level Midnight server. It has been invoked in dozens of subsequent conversations as shorthand for miscommunication, server drama, and the unexpected consequences of cheese-related discourse.

The incident is frequently cited as the reason the server developed a more self-aware, ironic culture going forward. It demonstrated, perhaps better than any other event, that in TLM, any topic — no matter how mundane — can become a Donnybrook.

Josh, who first called for clarification on January 21, 2023, has become the de facto keeper of the Kraftening's memory. His annual posts serve as both a reminder of the event and an implicit invitation to relitigate the cheese question — an invitation that, to date, no member has declined.
