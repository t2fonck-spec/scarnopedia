The Billcoin Economy

The Billcoin Economy is the internal economic system of Threat Level Midnight, managed through the The Bill Chain channel. It uses a proprietary currency — Billcoin — to gamify sports watching, exercise, prediction contests, and server participation, creating a parallel economy of achievement and competition layered over the server's natural social life.

Origins

The Billcoin economy appears to have grown organically from the server's sports-watching culture. Once members were already watching games together and discussing outcomes, the natural next step was to have something at stake. Billcoin provided that stake without requiring real money — a crucial distinction that kept the betting recreational rather than regulated.

Earning Mechanisms

Sports Betting

The primary Billcoin earning mechanism is sports betting. Members wager Billcoin on game outcomes, particularly in college football (Iowa games receive special attention) and other major sporting events. Bets are placed in the thousands of Billcoin, reflecting the scale of the economy and the confidence — or recklessness — of its participants.

Score Prediction Contests

Score prediction contests reward members for accurately predicting game scores rather than just outcomes. This system rewards analytical thinking and penalizes overconfidence — a nuanced distinction that suggests the economy's designers thought carefully about incentive structures.

Workout Rewards

The most philosophically interesting element of the Billcoin economy is the workout reward system. Members earn 500–1,000 Billcoin for completing workouts — converting real-world physical effort into digital currency. This creates a connection between the server economy and members' actual health, making Billcoin genuinely meaningful rather than purely abstract.

Booster Rewards

Monthly server boosts and first-time boost actions are tracked and rewarded with Billcoin, creating an additional incentive for members to invest in the server's growth.

The Wealth Gap: The Reed Situation

The Billcoin economy has produced at least one documented case of wealth inequality. A member named Reed was noted to have fallen to 9th place in the Billcoin rankings due to infrequent participation in the economy. This suggests the economy is meritocratic in the sense that consistent engagement is rewarded — but also that absence has real economic consequences, even in a fictional currency system.

Economic Philosophy

The Billcoin economy embodies a fundamentally optimistic view of community incentives: that members can be encouraged to exercise more, watch games more attentively, and invest more deeply in the server's success through the mechanism of a shared, transparent reward system. Whether Billcoin has actually caused members to exercise more is unverified, but the intention is admirable.
